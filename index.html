About Rakesh

The Story So Far...

Nicknamed as a “The Common Man's Comedian” for finding entertainment from day-to-day life and making people happy. Performed with famous comedians - Anubhav Bassi, Harsh Gujral, Jeeveshu Ahluwalia etc. Entertained popular brands like Coca-Cola, Flipkart, EY, Byju's, and others. He has also created a student fanbase in IIMs, DU, and many more.

He has participated in 2 National TV comedy shows with Sony Entertainment Television Network and also has regular appearances in Toastmasters International winning multiple speech contests.
